Atlassian App

Linked SLA Alerts

Notifies people when an SLA is at risk or breached. The app lives on the parent Jira issue that has the SLA; that issue can have linked issues. The app uses the parent’s SLA only—linked tickets don’t need their own SLA. Send alerts from the panel or on a schedule to linked tickets (comment + @mentions) and optional Slack or email.

  • Panel: “Send SLA Alert to Linked Tickets” — choose all or specific linked tickets, then post a comment on each with the parent’s SLA status and expiry; optional Slack with clickable Jira links
  • Scheduled alerts: when the parent’s SLA hits configured triggers (at risk, breached, or configurable time-left thresholds—e.g. 30 minutes remaining—with separate recipient sets per rule where supported), post a comment on each linked ticket and @mention that ticket’s assignee, reporter, watchers, and configurable fields (e.g. Request Participants)
  • Admin: triggers, who to @mention per trigger, Jira comment / Slack (webhook or Bot + channel) / email webhook, and two configurable message templates
  • Optional SLA field override; Slack token stored securely in Forge (masked, clearable)

Marketplace & support: Install from the Atlassian Marketplace (paid app). Product support from Tech Cache is by email—see Support & Security and Legal & Trust.
