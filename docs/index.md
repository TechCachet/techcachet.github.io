# Audit & Risk Insights: Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for Audit & Risk Insights. This guide will help you get started with your new Jira dashboard gadget.

## Getting Started

1. Navigate to your Jira Dashboard.
2. Click the 'Add gadget' button.
3. Search for 'Audit & Risk Insights' and add it to your dashboard.
4. Click the 'Edit' button on the gadget to configure your JQL filter and risk thresholds.

## Configuration Guide

The gadget configuration panel allows you to customize the following settings:

- JQL Query: Define the issues the gadget should analyze.
- Refresh interval: Set how often the data updates.
- Visible findings: Control how many risk cards are displayed.
- Thresholds: Define when an issue is considered stale, aging, or at SLA risk.

## Custom Risk Checks

You can add a custom JQL condition to monitor risk patterns specific to your organization. Once enabled, the gadget will display a custom card alongside standard insights.
