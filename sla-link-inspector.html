SLA Link Inspector

SLA Link Inspector is a Jira Cloud app that gives teams immediate visibility into the SLA status of linked issues. Instead of manually checking multiple tickets across different queues, the app displays SLA status directly inside the issue view.

Atlassian Forge App

Key Features

  • View SLA status for linked Jira issues directly in the issue panel
  • Color indicators for healthy, at-risk, and breached SLAs
  • Automatic alerts when linked tickets approach SLA risk
  • Configurable notification recipients and message templates
  • Optional integrations with Slack or email workflows

Why It Helps

Engineering and support teams often rely on work being completed in other queues before their own SLAs can be met. SLA Link Inspector helps teams see those risks earlier, giving engineers time to escalate or coordinate before deadlines are missed.
